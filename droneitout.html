Drone It Out Project (Feburary 2017 - Present)

I am currently stuying in the Software Engineering Department at San Jose State University. I am building an iOS app that allows me to control my DJI Phantom 3 Standard drone by voice commands. I plan to make most of basic commands for the drone by using Nuance's SpeechKit and DJI's Mobile-iOS SDK. With voice control, users can free up thier hands druing the flight. I am developing the app called DroneItOut in Xcode, using both Objective-C and Swift. By using DJI's Mobile-iOS SDK, I can implement most of functions of DJI Phantom 3, such as take off, land, go home, going to some directions using coordinates, waypoints mission, and follow me.
